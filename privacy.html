Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. Overview

Scandora ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our document scanning application available on iOS, macOS, and Android platforms.

By using Scandora, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account identification and communication)
  • Display name (optional, for personalization)
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed)

Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve our service:

  • Number of scans performed (anonymized counts)
  • Feature usage patterns (no document content)
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports (to fix bugs)

Document Data

Important: Scandora does NOT store your scanned documents on our servers. Your documents are:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Sent directly to your connected cloud services (Trello, Google Drive, etc.)
  • Temporarily processed by AI providers you choose (OpenAI, Google Gemini) if you enable AI features

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Scandora service
  • Authenticate your account and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service updates and security alerts
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

Your documents are NEVER stored on our infrastructure. They remain in your control via your connected cloud services.

5. Third-Party Services

Scandora integrates with third-party services based on your choices:

Cloud Integrations (Your Choice)

  • Trello: Documents and tasks sync to your Trello boards
  • Google Drive: Documents are uploaded to your Drive

AI Processing (Your Choice)

If you enable AI features, document content may be processed by:

  • OpenAI: For document analysis and data extraction
  • Google Gemini: For document analysis and data extraction

You can also bring your own API keys (BYOAI) for complete control over AI processing.

Analytics & Infrastructure

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Authentication, database, and analytics
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@scandora.eu.

Account Deletion: When you delete your Scandora account, we immediately and permanently remove ALL your personal data from our systems. This action is irreversible.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal, essential cookies for:

  • Language preferences
  • Session management

We do not use tracking cookies or sell your browsing data to advertisers.

8. Children's Privacy

Scandora is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Sending an email notification (for material changes)
  • Displaying an in-app notification

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Data Protection Contact

Email: privacy@scandora.eu

General Support: support@scandora.eu
